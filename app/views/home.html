How it Works

Teachers

Sign up, add students, and you're ready to go! You can manage your classrooms, assign grades online and track your students' attendance.

Students

Just sign up! You'll see the classrooms teachers have added you into on your dashboard. Your grades are one click away.

Parents

We know how smart your children are. But if you need any proof, then you can check out their grades online, any time.
